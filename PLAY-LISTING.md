## Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
3R Academy is a focused study companion for Nepal's health examinations — Public Service Commission (Loksewa), Council licensing (NMCLE and others) and entrance exams (MECEE-BL and more). Built by Dr. Mukesh Adhikari (PhD, MPH, MPA), it turns each chapter of the printed review books into fast, high-yield digital revision.

THE 3R METHOD
• Read — clean, high-yield notes: the important details only, no clutter.
• Recall — mnemonics, KEY facts and flashcards to lock it in.
• Rank — rationalised MCQs and real past questions to top the merit list.

WHAT YOU GET
• Flashcards with spaced repetition — rate each card Again / Good / Easy and the app resurfaces your weak cards at the right time (Anki-style), with a capped daily review so nothing piles up.
• Multiple-choice questions with real explanations — every option tells you WHY it is right or wrong, not just the answer. Practise in Study mode (instant feedback) or Timed mock-test mode (exam-style, with a full review at the end).
• Progress tracking — see cards reviewed, best quiz scores and what still needs work.
• Bookmark hard cards and questions, and auto-collect the ones you get wrong into a "weak areas" review.
• "Most important things to remember" — one-line rapid revision for the night before the exam.
• Video lessons per chapter (added over time).
• Works offline — study anywhere, no internet needed once a chapter is loaded.

FREE WITH YOUR BOOK
The app is free. Buy any 3R Academy review book, enter the access code printed inside, and unlock that book's full digital resources — for life. No subscription, no recurring fees.

EXAMS COVERED (growing)
• Loksewa (PSC): Health Assistant, Public Health Officer and more
• Licensing: Health Assistant, Staff Nurse (Council)
• Entrance: MBBS/BDS (MECEE-BL), BSc Nursing

PRIVACY
No account required. Your progress and access codes stay on your own device — nothing is collected or sold. See our privacy policy: https://3r.mukeshadhikari.com/privacy.html

Learn it · lock it in · top the merit list.
```
