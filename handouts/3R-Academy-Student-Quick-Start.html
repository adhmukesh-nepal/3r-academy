Student Quick-Start Guide

Public Health Officer · Loksewa — sign up and start practising in about 5 minutes.

1 Open & install the app

  • Open Chrome and go to 3r.mukeshadhikari.com.
  • Add to home screen on Android (Chrome):
    1. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top-right).
    2. Tap “Add to Home screen” — it may say “Install app.”
    3. Tap Add / Install → the 3R Academy icon appears on your home screen.
  • iPhone: open in Safari → Share → “Add to Home Screen.”
  • It then opens full-screen like an app and works offline.

2 Create your account

  • Tap Sign in (top-right) → “New here? Create an account.”
  • Enter your email + a password (6+ characters) → Create account.
  • Check your email for a code → enter it → Verify & sign in.
  • Fill the short profile: name, profession, exam, province, book. One sign-up unlocks everything, on any device.

3 Unlock your book

  • Open Public Health Officer — Loksewa.
  • On “Unlock your resources,” type the access code → Unlock. You only do this once per device.

4 Track your progress

  • Your scores, starred items, and review schedule save on your device (and sync if signed in).
  • Study a little every day to keep your daily streak 🔥 going.
  • Suggested order: Scale & data types → Data presentation → Central tendency → Dispersion.
📚 Inside each chapter — three ways to learn

Read — Key points

The “most important things to remember.” Skim these first to get the big picture.

Recall — Flashcards

Tap a card to flip it, then rate how well you knew it — hard cards come back sooner. Tap ★ Star to save tricky ones.

Rank — MCQs

Study mode: see the answer & why each option is right/wrong. Timed test: 45 sec per question, score at the end.

Best way to study: do a Study-mode run first to learn, then a Timed test to check yourself under exam pressure. Afterwards, use Review weak areas and Starred to focus on what you missed.
No internet? Once a chapter has opened, it keeps working offline. Forgot your password? Use “Email me a code” on the sign-in screen.
